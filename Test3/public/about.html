MTU Scheduling App

This is our MTU Adaptive Scheduling App, designed to be used by CS students at MTU. This app was designed and developed over an 8-week portion of CS3141 (Team Software Project) by the below students


Stay Tuned for the below features!

- Removing a course that the system assumes you have taken but you have not
- Searched courses limited to only courses for which the prereqs have been satisfied
- Suggested courses towards the top of the searched courses
- Manually added courses in "Course Worksapce" saved even after switching year/semester
